# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you find a security bug, please report it privately using GitHub's **Private Vulnerability Reporting** feature. Please do not open a public issue for it.

## Disclosure

Please give us time to fix the bug before you publish your write-up or Proof of Concept. Once the patch is pushed to the repository, feel free to share your findings publicly.

## Scope

This policy applies to **Lebirun** and its main components, including `leblibc` and `lebutils`.
